## About Me

<span class='anchor' id='about-me'></span>

Kaiqiang Lin received his B.Eng. degree in Electronic and Information Engineering from Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University (FAFU), Fuzhou, China, in 2018, and his Ph.D. degree with the College of Surveying and Geo-Informatics Tongji University (Top 8 in China mainland, QS rankings 2025), Shanghai, China, in 2024, under the supervision of [Prof. Tong Hao](https://celiang.tongji.edu.cn/info/1300/2387.htm). From 2021 to 2023, he was a visiting Ph.D. student at the Center for Wireless Communications, University of Oulu, Oulu, Finland, under the supervision of [Prof. Hirley Alves]((https://sites.google.com/view/hirley-alves). He is currently a postdoc research fellow with the Communication Theory Lab at King Abdullah University of Science and Technology under the supervision of [Prof. Mohamed-Slim Alouini](https://www.kaust.edu.sa/en/study/faculty/mohamed-slim-alouini).
